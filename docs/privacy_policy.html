Effective date: March 2026

LOIRELAB GAMES ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile games, including Clean the Earth. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our applications.

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Device information (device model, OS version, language, region)
  • Unique identifiers (advertising ID, Firebase instance ID)
  • Gameplay data (progress, session length, in-game actions)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve gameplay, balance, and user experience

3. Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics (GA4) to analyze gameplay and app performance. Data collected is aggregated and does not directly identify individual users.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data such as advertising ID to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

5. In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store payment information.

6. Legal Basis (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on:

  • Legitimate interest (improving the game and stability)

7. User Consent

Where required, we request your consent for data collection and personalized ads in accordance with Google’s EU User Consent Policy. You can change your preferences in device settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain anonymized analytics data as long as necessary to operate and improve the game.
